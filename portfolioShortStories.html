Canis Lupus

Written: 2021
Author's Comment: We were tasked to write a short story with a word constraint based off the Fibonacci sequence. For example, our first two lines had to be 1 word long and the third had to be 2 words following the Fibonacci sequence. This assignment came at a convienent time because it allowed me to work through some emotions I was going through and I am proud of the finished product.
