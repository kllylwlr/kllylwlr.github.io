About

Hello!

My name is Kelly Lawlor and I am a Senior studying English and Computer Science at New York University.


I have loved to exercise my creativity from a young age, and I was drawn by UX because unlike other design fields where you aim to create the most aesthetically pleasing product, in UX you must also work to create an enjoyable and painless experience, and the challenge of striking that perfect balance between form and function along with having the opportunity to improve people’s day are something I find very rewarding.


Outside of work I enjoy engaging in a lot of other creative hobbies such as sewing, drawing, and writing!
