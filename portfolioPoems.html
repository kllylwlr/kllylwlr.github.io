Dream, Part II

Written: 2019

The Lonely Girl

Written: 2017
Author's Comment: When I wrote this I intended the narrator to seem relieved when the person they were waiting for came home, but when I presented this to my writing class many of them thought the narrator was afraid of the person coming home. Rereading this I don't know what I was talking about because it definitely comes off as the latter.

Author's Comment: My creative writing teacher gave us a template which was essentially to write "I am from" and then a series of different nouns. I remember not being very fond of this when I initially wrote it but coming back to it I have a new appreciation for it.
